Global Digital Adoption in Fire Academies

Around the world, fire academies are shifting from paper-based systems to digital training ecosystems.

  • Sweden: Distance learning improved flexibility & digital visibility.
  • United States: LMS platforms standardised training at scale.
  • United Kingdom: Blended learning reduced skill fade & cost.
  • Global Trend: VR & simulation for hazardous scenario training.
